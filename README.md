# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project transforms raw retail transaction data into an interactive Excel dashboard. The analysis focuses on understanding revenue drivers, customer demographics (age/gender), and monthly sales cycles to provide actionable business recommendations.

## 📊 Key Insights
* **Revenue Leader:** Electronics is the top-performing category, generating over ₦155,000.
* **Volume vs. Value:** While Electronics earns the most revenue, **Clothing** is the most frequently purchased item across all genders.
* **Core Customer:** The **48–57 age bracket** is the most active consumer group.
* **Gender Split:** The market is balanced, with a near 50/50 split between Male and Female shoppers.
* **Sales Trend:** Analysis reveals a steady decline in sales from a peak in **May** through to **September**, indicating a need for mid-year promotional interventions.

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
* **Data Cleaning:** Standardized currency (₦), handled missing values, and categorized age groups.
* **Pivot Tables:** Aggregated 1,000+ rows of data to calculate gender ratios and product totals.
* **Advanced Formulas:** Utilized `SUMIFS` for revenue tracking and `XLOOKUP` for data mapping.
* **Visual Design:** Developed an interactive UI featuring:
    * Clustered Bar Charts (Product vs. Gender)
    * Donut Charts (Gender Ratio)
    * Trend Lines (Monthly Performance)

## 💡 Recommendations
1. **Seasonal Campaigns:** Implement a "Mid-Year Sale" in July/August to counteract the observed seasonal slump.
2. **Targeted Marketing:** Focus high-ticket Electronic ads toward the 48-57 age demographic.
3. **Product Bundling:** Bundle low-volume Beauty products with high-volume Clothing items to increase Average Order Value.
